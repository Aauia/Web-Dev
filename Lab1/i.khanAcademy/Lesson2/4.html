About Bora Bora

Bora Bora, located in French Polynesia, is known for its crystal-clear waters, overwater bungalows, and lush mountain landscapes. This tropical paradise offers visitors an escape from the ordinary, with world-class snorkeling, romantic sunsets, and serene beaches.

Things to Do

  • Snorkel with rays and sharks in the lagoon.
  • Take a romantic sunset cruise.
  • Relax on Matira Beach, one of the best beaches in the world.
  • Hike Mount Otemanu for stunning views.
  • Indulge in Polynesian cuisine and culture.
